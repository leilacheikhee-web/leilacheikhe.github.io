About Me

From Morocco — to the World

I grew up in Dakhla — a city on the Atlantic coast of Morocco, far from any startup hub or tech campus. Most people cannot find it on a map. That distance never felt like a limitation. It felt like an opportunity.

While others waited for technology to reach them, I went to meet it. I taught myself artificial intelligence, fullstack engineering, cybersecurity, and business strategy — because I saw what was missing and decided I would be the one to build it.

Africa has 1.3 billion people. Most AI tools are not built for them. Almost none are built by them. I am changing that — one project at a time, in Arabic, French, and English, from Morocco.
